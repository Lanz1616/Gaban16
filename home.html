Adobong Manok

Ingredients

  • Chicken thighs
  • Garlic
  • Oyster sauce
  • Dark soy sauce
  • White vinegar
  • Light brown sugar
  • Bay leaves and black peppercorns

Cooking Procedures

  • Heat the oil in a cooking pot.
  • Add the garlic. Cook until it starts to turn light brown.
  • Add the peppercorns and bay leaves. Continue to cook for 20 seconds so that its flavors get infused in the oil.
  • Put the chicken in the cooking pot. Stir and cook until it turns light brown. Note: check the garlic and make sure that it does not get burnt. Adjust heat if necessary.
  • Pour the soy sauce and vinegar. Let boil. Cover and cook in low heat for 40 minutes or until the chicken gets tender. Add more water if the liquid starts to dry quickly.
  • Taste the chicken adobo and decide to add salt if needed.
